THE FIFTH LABEL

The Fifth Label is an Australian women's clothing brand morphing high fashion with bold streetwear tailored for the modern era.

Since its launch in June 2014 by an Australian designer Zoey Vink, The Fifth Label has garnered a cult following for uncomplicated, confident everyday wear.

Because life is busy enough The Fifth Label emanates relaxed versatility.

Focusing on affordability without compromising quality, The Fifth Label uses unstructured fabrications that are easy wash & easy wear - original prints and an accessible color palette.
