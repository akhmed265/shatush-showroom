FINDERS KEEPERS

Since early beginnings in 2007, Finders Keepers has developed its effortlessly sexy aesthetic from simple party wear to must-have ‘it girl’ style.

Designed to take the wearer anywhere she should choose to go, each piece features its own fun, flirty element unique to the brand - items that become staples in every wardrobe, with their flattering and versatile shapes.

Any of the eleven collections that Finders Keepers produce annually offer a carefully curated range of styles for all occasions.

Each collection will have a print, shape or texture to cater for casual daywear through to playfully sexy evening looks.

Unsurprisingly, this versatility of Finders Keepers has seen the label stocked in some of the most prestigious retailers worldwide.
