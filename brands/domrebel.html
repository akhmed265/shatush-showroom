DOMREBEL

DOMREBEL is short for "DOMINUS REBELLIS", which in Latin means "MASTER OF REBELLION".

It is a visual arts company based in Montreal that specializes in hand-crafted graphic tees.

Their products are being sold in luxury stores in 35 countries around the world and make special custom pieces for GUNS N' ROSES, U2, RIHANNA, JAMIE FOXX, TIESTO, CHRIS BROWN, ELTON JOHN + MORE.

DOMREBEL was founded in 2003 in Montréal by two university students, Justin Svatina and Don Nguyen.

They focused their efforts on making the best hand-made t-shirts they could.

Their tees were screen printed by hand, vintage washed in their parents' laundry machines, paint splattered and hand-studded with crystals.

Even if they weren't to everyone's taste, those t-shirts got people in Montréal, and eventually Canada and the world, talking and paying attention.
