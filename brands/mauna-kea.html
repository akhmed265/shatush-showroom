MAUNA-KEA

Mauna-Kea is a brand created in Italy that suddenly became a commercial success in the 90s for the sportsmanship, characterized by technical fabrics and multi-color prints.

The mission of Mauna-Kea is to find a new way of seeing fashion using new unusual materials.

Mauna-Kea breaks the border between man and woman, formal and informal, between active and ready-to-wear design.

The desire to experiment with new construction techniques such as applying elements printed in 3D, new dyes, washes and finishing techniques, nourishes the brand’s soul.
