Cotton Citizen

Driven by Founder and Creative Director Adam Vanunu, COTTON CITIZEN is about experimenting with colour, mastering the art of garment dying, setting colour trends and perfecting the exacting fits and hand - distressed finishes on the brand’s timeless cotton basics.

A curated palette of super saturated, eye-catching colours are released every season, created exclusively with their dye house’s capabilities.

Inspired by destinations around the world, Cotton Citizen is designed and produced in Los Angeles.

Each Cotton Citizen piece is as unique as the person who wears it.
