STORM & MARIE

STORM & MARIE was established in Copenhagen in 2010 by Signe Elisabeth Vedel and Kim Vedel Hansen.

The Danish brand is the result of a shared vision of the two who form a couple both professionally and privately.

The philosophy behind the brand is that while every collection forms a concept, each piece can still be worn separately and effortlessly mixed in different ways, to create an individual style.

Clean Scandinavian lines are combined with appealing prints and textures.

A focus on high quality and fit are the core of the 4 yearly STORM & MARIE collections created by designer Signe Elisabeth Vedel.
