SOIA & KYO

Launched in 2004, SOIA & KYO is a Montreal based brand who prides itself on being a strong supporter of local talent and offers exclusive styles all designed in Canada.

Ilan Elfassy, creative director and founder of SOIA & KYO, has over 20 years of experience in the clothing industry. The combination of his knowledge in both retail and manufacturing brings a great asset to the brand.

Since its debut, SOIA & KYO has been offering quality, fit, versatility, innovation and accessibility.

Over the years, the label has evolved in order to meet the needs of its growing clientele.

In 2010, SOIA & KYO introduced a men’s line with the commitment to bring the quality and features of European designs to North America.

From its humble beginnings, SOIA & KYO now reaches a wide audience with its clean and modern tailoring.

The collection for both men and women integrate sophisticated looks and redefine sportswear with a strong focus on fabrics and subtle details.

The brand offers a range of classic trenches, soft suede and urban leathers in addition to a variety of wools, downs and rainwear.

Simply put, SOIA & KYO jackets are designed to provide the perfect balance between fashion and comfort.
