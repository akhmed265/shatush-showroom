ELLIATT

After working for a number of Australian fashion brands, Katie Pratt saw a gap in the Australian market for unique, beautiful designer womenswear.

Pratt launched ELLIATT from her home in Australia in 2011.

ELLIATT specialises in fine detailing such as embroidery, laces and specialty fabrications - all of which are high-quality product.

Strong feminine shapes paired with innovative embellishment have since become key to the recognisable handwriting of the ELLIATT brand.

Now ELLIATT has since grown globally, and is stocked across 6 continents in more than 1200 boutiques around the world.
