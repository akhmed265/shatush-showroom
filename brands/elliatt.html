ELLIATT

After working for a number of Australian fashion brands, Katie Pratt saw a gap in the Australian market for unique, beautiful designer womenswear.

launched ELLIATT from her home in Australia in 2011.

specialises in fine detailing such as embroidery, laces and specialty fabrications - all of which are high-quality product.

feminine shapes paired with innovative embellishment have since become key to the recognisable handwriting of the ELLIATT brand.

ELLIATT has since grown globally, and is stocked across 6 continents in more than 1200 boutiques around the world.
