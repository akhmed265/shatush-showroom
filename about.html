About us

Shatush is a fashion multi-brand platform that brings together contemporary designers and fashion buyers from Eastern European countries (Russia, Ukraine, Azerbaijan etc ) in its showroom in Milan since 2010.

Our mission is to create a lively network between brands and fashion buyers from various boutiques, department stores, and online shops.

Providing an accurate sales service and full customer support we have reached a high level of communication between our customers and the brands we represent.

Our knowledge, experience and a forward-looking approach to our business allow us to meet the demands of both our clients and fashion designers, respecting their choices and preferences.
